Tunis Business School, a pioneering institution at the University of Tunis that has been shaping futures since its inception on October 25th, 2010.
As the first and only public business school in Tunisia to utilize English as its primary language of instruction and adopt the US higher education academic system, Tunis Business School stands as a beacon of innovation and global learning.
The school is situated in Tunis, the capital of Tunisia.

How to Reach the Venue

By Bus

You can purchase single or multiple bus tickets with cash at the bus station

By Metro

You can take metro number 1 from the Barcelona metro station in Tunis, which will take you to the metro station in Mourouj 4.
From there, you can take a taxi to reach TBS, with the fare approximately 4 TND (around 1.3 USD).

You can purchase single or multiple metro tickets with cash at the Barcelona metro station.

By Taxi

You can take a taxi from anywhere to reach TBS. From the city center of Tunis, the fare is approximately 12 TND (around 3.9 USD)

By Chauffeured Car / Car-Sharing

You can use websites like coccinelle to rent a car or minibus with a chauffeur, either for yourself or to share with others.
