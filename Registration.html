Registration Details

We are pleased to announce that we are sponsoring registration fees to waive them and allow students in this low-income country to benefit from this event free of charge.
Our goal is to allow 60 students to participate. However, we will still review applications and select students who are likely to benefit from the event based on elitism and diversity.

Selected students will benefit from:

  • Access to all sessions
  • Lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet
  • T-shirt, notepad, and pen, sponsored by the University of Michigan-Flint
  • A certificate of participation for the “IEEE CIS Summer School”
